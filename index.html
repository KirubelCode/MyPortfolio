Sharks and Fish Simulation

A multi-threaded Wa-Tor simulation built in Go, modeling predator-prey interactions between sharks and fish. The project analyses the effects of concurrency on performance, comparing threaded and non-threaded execution across different grid sizes. Results highlight how workload distribution, synchronisation overhead, and CPU core limitations impact speedup efficiency.
